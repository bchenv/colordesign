Good Example


The Eleven Plants for Dum-Dums and Cool People website uses a bunch of different shades of green and some brown colors on a light cream background. Green is cool because it reminds people of nature and being outdoors, which is great if you're selling natural stuff. Brown is often used with green for things like food and farming, making it feel natural and organic. So, these colors together give off a chill vibe and make the website feel connected to nature and all that good stuff.


The Cantina Valpolicella Negrar website has a cozy feel because it uses earthy colors like brown and green. These colors make you feel grounded and connected to nature, which is perfect for a wine company because wine comes from grapes grown in nature. If you're into nature and sustainability, using colors like brown and green is a great idea for your website and brand because they give off that natural vibe.


The Collagerie website looks really cool because it uses colors like lilac and cream. These colors give off a fresh and peaceful vibe, which is perfect for a fashion website targeting younger people. If you want your website to feel calm and appealing to a younger crowd, using colors like lilac and cream could be a good idea.
